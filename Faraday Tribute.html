Michael Faraday is known as one of the most influential scientist of all time. What I respect about him the most is that he was self-taught.

He grew up poor and was even malnourished and not able to continue his education.

Faraday worked as an apprentice in a book-binding shop where he grew a love for reading and consuming knowledge. Reading "Improvement of the Mind" by Isaac Watts is what influenced him to a dedicated life of discovery.

Faraday did not get very far interms of standard education; although, his inventions continue to influence modern day science and engineering.
